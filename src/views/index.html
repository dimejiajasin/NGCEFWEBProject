The Vision

New Generation Change Education Foundation's vision is focused towards creating a new National Ethos that will galvanize visioary leadership and patriotic followership across all sectors of Nigerian populace into positive attitudinal reformation "in order to foster a United, Secure, Prosperous and Economically viable Nigeria".

The Mision

The mission of NGCEF is to establish a National Moral Rebirth and Character Reformation that would change the attitudes of every citizen of Nigeria by aligning their everyday conduct with the Core Values clearly set out in Chapter ii, Section 23 of the 1999 Constitution of the Federal Republic of Nigeria, which states, "The National ethics shall be Discipline, Integrity, Dignity of Labour, Social justice, Religion tolerance, Self reliance and Patriotism", thereby realizing our vision of 'changing man from the inside out!'.

OUR STRATEGY

NGCEF's strategy is drama based and learner centered nurturing an all round development of our target audience, Nigerians, categorized under Primary/Secondary schools, Tertiary, Youth Corps members and the Workforce.

Primary/ Secondary/ Tertiary

Drama based scripts using the 7 core values for Social Studies in the Primary Schools and Civics in Secondary Schools, acted out by the pupils themselves. General Staudies at the 100 level at the Universities during which Core Values are revisited through Drama.

NYSC

A post-tertiary initiative targeted at the Youth Corpers to revisit Nigeria’s Core Values during the Orientation Camps as well as apply relevant ones during Community Service.

Workforce

A 30 minutes serial T.V Docu-drama targeted at the parents, to tackle Nigerias Core Values, vis-a-vis their application at work. Screening would be on National Television Network(T.V./Radio) at prime time.
